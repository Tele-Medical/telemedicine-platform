# Rural Telemedicine Platform Design System

## Core Aesthetic
- **Vibe:** Calm, trusting, human-centric, very modern, premium, offline-first. Not sterile or clinical.

## Colors
- **Primary:** #6B8E7B
- **Secondary:** #4A8F96
- **Background:** #F9F8F6
- **Surfaces:** #FFFFFF
- **Text Primary:** #2D3748
- **Text Secondary:** #4A5568
- **Success/Sync:** #38A169
- **Warning/Offline:** #D69E2E

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter
- **Body Size:** 16px default for high legibility.

## UI Elements
- **Elevation:** Use soft, diffused drop shadows (shadow-lg, shadow-soft).
- **Shapes:** Large touch targets, highly rounded corners (rounded-2xl for cards, rounded-full for buttons).
- **Effects:** Light use of glassmorphism (translucency and background blur) for sticky headers and bottom navs.
